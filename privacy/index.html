Sunny Pubs

Privacy Policy

Sunny Pubs helps people find pubs with sun at the right time. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how analytics cookies are used when you allow them.

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What we collect

We may collect location-related inputs you actively use in the app, feedback you submit about pubs or weather conditions, and limited technical data needed to run the service safely and reliably.

Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand which pages and features are being used. This helps us improve the app, fix issues, and decide what to build next. If you do not accept analytics, those tracking events are not sent.

User feedback

When you submit feedback about a pub, outdoor area, weather, or sunshine status, we store that information so it can be reviewed and used to improve the quality of the app’s data.

Third-party services

Sunny Pubs may rely on trusted third-party services for analytics, weather, mapping, and infrastructure.

  • Google Analytics for usage analytics when consent is granted
  • Map and weather providers to power the core experience
  • Supabase and related infrastructure for feedback storage and app data workflows

Your choices

You can choose whether to allow analytics cookies from the consent banner. Closing the banner does not grant consent. If needed, we can later add a permanent privacy settings control in the app.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Sunny Pubs, please contact the site owner through the public project contact channels linked from the app or repository.
