Sunny Pubs

Privacy Policy

Sunny Pubs helps people find pubs with sun at the right time. This policy explains what data Sunny Pubs and developer Felipe de Uy collect, why it is used, and how optional privacy and cookie choices work.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Data we handle

Location is used only when you tap the location button. The app may process approximate or precise location to show nearby sunny pubs and local weather, but it does not request background location. We also handle feedback submissions, local app preferences, analytics data if you opt in, and technical or security data needed to keep the service reliable.

Why we use data

Data supports map, search, location, weather, pub feedback, weather feedback review, app reliability, security, and product improvement. Optional analytics is used only to understand aggregate usage and improve the app.

Privacy and cookie choices

Essential app storage is always on because it is required for routing, security, local queues, and core app operation. Optional Preferences can remember safe convenience choices on this device. Optional Analytics controls whether Google Analytics loads and whether analytics page views or events are sent. Marketing and ads are not used, and ad storage, ad user data, and ad personalization stay denied.

Third-party services

Sunny Pubs may rely on trusted third-party services for analytics, weather, mapping, feedback, and infrastructure.

  • Google Analytics for usage analytics only when Analytics is granted
  • Map and weather providers for the core map, search, location, and weather experience
  • Supabase and related infrastructure for feedback storage and app data workflows

Retention and deletion

Local preferences stay on your device until you change or clear them. Feedback is retained for data quality review. Privacy or deletion requests can be sent through the Sunny Pubs GitHub repository at github.com/felps85/sunny-dublin/issues or through the app store contact route where the app is listed.

Your choices

You can change optional Preferences and Analytics any time from Privacy & cookie choices in the app. Rejecting Analytics prevents Google Analytics from loading and stops analytics page views or events.

Store notes

Sunny Pubs does not sell personal or sensitive data. It has no ads, no marketing tracking, no owner billing, and no native checkout in v1. If account creation is later added to native apps, account deletion must be added before store submission.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through the GitHub repository issue route above or through the store listing contact route for the version of Sunny Pubs you use.
